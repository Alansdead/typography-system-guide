Comprehensive guide for implementing accessible typography in mobile apps – (V 3.1)

iOS Typography System

Apple's San Francisco font family with Dynamic Type support for accessibility. Automatically switches between SF Pro Text for smaller sizes (≤19pt) and SF Pro Display for larger sizes (≥20pt). As of iOS 18, Dynamic Type supports 12 sizes — xSmall through xxxLarge plus five Larger Accessibility Sizes (AX1–AX5) — and body text can scale up to 312% at AX5.

Android Typography System

  • This guide recommends a minimum of 16sp for body text readability; Material Design 3’s official minimum is 14sp.
